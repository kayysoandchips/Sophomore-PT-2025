Welcome to Botanical Whiz—The Ultimate Flower Guessing Challenge!
Are you ready to test your botanical knowledge? Whether you're a flower fanatic or just starting to explore the world of plants, Botanical Whiz is here to challenge you! In this fun and engaging game, you'll be given a series of flower images, and your task is to guess their names. Sounds easy, right? But don’t be fooled—the game features two exciting modes to suit every skill level!

  • Easy Mode: Perfect for beginners or anyone looking for a relaxed, educational experience. Test your knowledge of common flowers and take your time to learn new ones.

  • Hard Mode: For the true flower experts! Here, you’ll face more obscure plants and tougher challenges. Only the most seasoned botanical whizzes will thrive! Ready to prove you’re a flower expert? Let’s get started and see how well you know the world of plants!
